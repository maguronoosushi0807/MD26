Every university students in Japan should be required to study abroad for at least one semester.
Do you agree or disagree? Give two reasons to support your idea.

主張: 1学期以上海外留学を強制するのは反対だ。
理由1: 海外留学はお金がかかって、バイトしないといけない人が困る
理由2: 外国人とコミュニケーションを取るならオンラインや留学生で十分

I disagree with the opinion that every university students in Japan should be required to study abroad for at least one semester.
One reason is that studying abroad burdens most students heavily. Expenses for flight tickets and hotels are very high, and these costs violate educational opportunities especially from students who have to work part-time for educational expenses.
Another reason is that students can communicate in foreign languages in Japan. By using online service, such as zoom, or communicating with foreign people who come to Japan to study, students can study foreign languages and culture.
